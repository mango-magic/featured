Highlight Reel

The moments that hit

Twenty-seven moments from the conversation on mangrove planning, carbon credits funding, drone-based planting, and the Proof of Tree platform.

Clip 1

One Day Initiative (Ethiopia): 29 million people united to plant 714 million trees in 24 hours, setting a record and pushing toward the goal of one billion trees, with the Grolympic Games highlighting impact.

Clip 2

Guinness World Record holder for most trees planted in 24 hours: over 30,000 mangrove trees planted, with lighthearted 'secrets' behind the remarkable feat.

Clip 3

Australia's bushfires' devastating impact on wildlife: alarming animal death numbers and biodiversity loss, underscoring the urgent need for action.

Clip 4

TreeGens and carbon credits: a $400K investment to preserve the Amazon and fund reforestation, signaling a forward-looking approach to carbon offsetting.

Clip 5

CORSIA credits and a startup's historic deal: funding that can plant more trees and unlock opportunities in a multi-billion-dollar market.

Clip 6

Australia vs. Ethiopia: bushfires and animal loss, exploring whether Australia can match Ethiopia's tree-planting scale given labor and land challenges.
