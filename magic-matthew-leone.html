Automation Workflows

Matthew's automation magic

Ten powerful AI-driven workflows that Matthew discussed, transforming how businesses operate from sales to customer engagement.

Automation 1
Cost of Goods Sold Calculation
Google Sheets→ OpenAI API→ Google Sheets→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Logger→ Backup
Automates cost adjustment calculations for new product line features using AI, enabling quicker decision-making.
Reduces reliance on finance teams for basic calculations and accelerates response time.
Automation 2
Dynamic Reporting Generation
Google Sheets→ OpenAI NLP→ Data Retrieval→ Report Generator→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Save
Automatically generates customized reports based on user queries, significantly streamlining the reporting process.
Eliminates manual report creation and increases efficiency for sales teams.
Automation 3
Genius Voice Assist Integration
Voice Input→ Voice Recognition→ OpenAI Intent→ DB Query→ Response Gen→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC
Enables users to interact with the CRM using voice commands to access appointment details quickly and efficiently.
Enhances user experience by providing immediate, hands-free access to information.
Automation 4
Inventory Matching via Natural Language
User Input→ OpenAI NLP→ Inventory DB→ Formatter→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Log
Finds the best vehicle matches based on user-provided criteria through natural language queries, improving the sales process.
Reduces search time for sales personnel and optimizes customer engagement with accurate vehicle matches.
Automation 5
Automated Customer Follow-ups
CRM Trigger→ OpenAI Draft→ Email→ Slack→ QC→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ Log→ SMS→ Webhooks
Automates the follow-up process with new customers, ensuring timely communication and engagement.
Minimizes manual follow-up efforts, increasing the chances of converting leads into sales.
Automation 6
Lead Scoring and Assignment
CRM Trigger→ OpenAI Score→ DB Update→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Log→ Webhooks
Scores leads based on defined criteria and assigns them to the appropriate sales representative automatically.
Improves lead management efficiency and ensures timely responses to potential customers.
Automation 7
AI-Driven Customer Profiling
CRM Data→ OpenAI Analyze→ DB Update→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Backup→ Webhooks
Analyzes customer data to update profiles and provide insights for targeted marketing strategies.
Enhances marketing effectiveness by ensuring the right campaigns reach the right audience based on behavior analysis.
Automation 8
Real-time Sales Data Logging
Sales Trigger→ API Call→ DB Log→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Daily Backup→ Webhooks
Logs sales data in real-time for accurate tracking and reporting purposes.
Reduces errors in sales data collection and improves visibility into sales performance.
Automation 9
Marketing Campaign Optimization
Campaign Trigger→ OpenAI Analyze→ Engagement Data→ Recommendations→ Email→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Log
Automatically analyzes campaign performance and suggests optimizations based on customer engagement data.
Increases the effectiveness of marketing activities by ensuring data-driven adjustments.
Automation 10
Customer Feedback Gathering
CRM Trigger→ Email Request→ DB Log→ OpenAI Analyze→ Slack→ Formatter→ Error Handler→ QC→ Store→ Webhooks
Automates the gathering, analysis, and logging of customer feedback post-purchase to inform product improvements.
Enhances customer satisfaction and product quality through timely feedback integration.
